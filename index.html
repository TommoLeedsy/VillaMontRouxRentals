The Villa

Set in the private domain of Val Grimaud, the villa is the ideal location for a relaxing holiday in picturesque Provence. The peaceful villages of Gassin and Ramutuelle and the bustling town of St Tropez, with its famous beaches are all within easy reach (15 minute drive to St. Tropez, 10 minutes to the beach). The villa is well equipped with 5 bedrooms, set in an acre of beautiful well maintained gardens with its own private swimming pool.

Recently refurbished to a high standard, giving all the comforts of home. Situated on a peaceful hillside with breath taking views of the Gulf of St Tropez and the local countryside and a short walk from the ancient village of Grimaud or just minutes by car. There are two private shared tennis courts and a boules square within the domain.
