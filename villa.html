The Villa

The villa sleeps a maximum of 10. It comprises of 4 bedrooms in the main house - 2 double bedrooms, one with twin beds, a room with bunk beds plus two bathrooms with separate toilets. Adjoining the house is a double studio with ensuite bathroom and its own entrance, perfect for an au pair, teenagers or grand parents who like some independence.
There is a large spacious living and dining area leading onto the terrace through French doors. The sitting room is fully equipped with LCD TV, VCR, DVD and English TV channels through a Sky box.
The kitchen is equipped to a high standard with a Bosch dishwasher, gas hob and electric oven, fridge freezer and microwave. There are even plastic plates for the children and outdoor glasses for drinking by the pool. In the cellar there is an extra fridge freezer and a washing machine
Having children of our ownwe have tried to make the villa as child friendly as possible for that most relaxing holiday. The villa includes a cot, highchair and a range of toys, books, DVDs and videos. So there's no need to carry all that extra luggage.
There is a secure gated entrance and parking for several cars. The house is fully alarmed for total security.
